What is Our Music Time?

Our Music Time is a bilingual and Kodály-inspired teaching course incorporating flexible lesson-planning and song-leading structures for teaching general music classes and private instrumental lessons, especially piano.

Instructors Database

Yu-Pei Hsiao

Yu-Pei Hsiao is currently the Director of Graz Music and Chairperson of the Taiwan Science and Arts Education Association. She specializes in teaching piano, violin, and orchestral conducting, with many years of teaching experience. Under her leadership, the Graz Music team has achieved outstanding results in student admissions to music programs, competitions, and ensemble training. She is also dedicated to promoting music education for children in remote areas, aiming to improve musical literacy among underprivileged students. Ms. Yu-Pei introduced the Our Music Time curriculum, which is based on the Kodály Method. Through this program, her students have been able to build a strong musical foundation. The curriculum has proven highly effective in both individual and group instruction, greatly enhancing students’ overall music learning and development.

Shuo-Li Hung

Shuo-Li Hung holds a Master’s degree in Piano from National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University. Since 2010, she has been professionally teaching piano and violin. Ms. Shuo-Li is currently devoted to music education and various chamber music collaborations in southern Taiwan, and has founded a rare dual-piano performance hall in Kaohsiung. In her teaching, She maintains a lively and engaging style, aiming to cultivate the musical abilities of Taiwanese students. Ms. Shou-Li introduced the Our Music Time curriculum from the United States, which is based on the Kodály Method, using specially designed teaching tools along with Chinese and English folk song materials to nurture musical literacy and inspire students to joyfully explore the world of music.
