About Our Music Time

What is Our Music Time?

OUR MUSIC TIME was founded in 2025 by Chiunglien Liu in San Jose, California. She is the CEO of OUR MUSIC TIME and the author of numerous books. In the 2000s, Chiunglien combined Eastern and Western cultures with her decades of teaching experience and pedagogical research, creating a solid foundation for students to learn music, particularly musicianship. Chiunglien's work gradually evolved to include the sharing of engaging and challenging activities with music educators. Her approach not only motivated teachers but also inspired students to immerse themselves in their instrument learning. By stimulating both the right and left hemispheres of the brain, Chiunglien's methods encourage physical engagement with music, fostering a holistic learning environment that emphasizes creativity alongside technical skill development.

Our Publications

OUR MUSIC TIME has developed a spiral curriculum ("Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced") for music teachers, designed to support both group and individual instrumental lessons. Inspired by the Kodály method, these materials aim to cultivate lifelong musical skills in children. The program encourages incorporating practical singing and listening exercises into existing lessons, following Kodály's three-step approach: Prepare, Present, and Practice.

Supplementary Teaching Materials

OUR MUSIC TIME complete Supplementary Teaching Materials include Song Picture Magnets, Colorful Picture Cards, and Song Lyric Posters.

Have you wondered how to teach pitch and rhythm skills practically? Pitch and rhythm are abstract and complex concepts, but this significant resource of 'Song Picture Magnet' makes the concepts of duration and rhythm less abstract, more fun, and more concrete. Teach your children to feel the pitch and rhythm even on the staff by singing selected folk songs with Colorful Picture Cards and Song Lyric Posters. They are multi-level resources and great for both individual and group classes.

Song Picture Magnets

  • 73 song and rhyme magnets for learning pitch and rhythm
  • Two sizes relative to note values for easy visualization
  • 5cm and 2.5cm heights for easy handling
  • 20 Heart-shaped magnets for steady beat practice
  • Includes quarter rest, repeat sign magnets, and canvas bags

Colorful Picture Cards

  • 89 colorful cards for musical theory learning
  • Musical Alphabet and Solfège integration
  • Kodály sequence rhythm patterns

Yu-Pei Hsiao

Yu-Pei Hsiao is currently the Director of Graz Music and Chairperson of the Taiwan Science and Arts Education Association. She specializes in teaching piano, violin, and orchestral conducting, with many years of teaching experience. Under her leadership, the Graz Music team has achieved outstanding results in student admissions to music programs, competitions, and ensemble training. She is also dedicated to promoting music education for children in remote areas, aiming to improve musical literacy among underprivileged students. Ms. Yu-Pei introduced the Our Music Time curriculum, which is based on the Kodály Method. Through this program, her students have been able to build a strong musical foundation. The curriculum has proven highly effective in both individual and group instruction, greatly enhancing students' overall music learning and development.
