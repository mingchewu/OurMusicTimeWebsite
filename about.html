About Chunglien Liu

Founder, Our Music Time

Chunglien Liu is a passionate music educator, researcher, and founder of Our Music Time. With decades of experience integrating Eastern and Western pedagogical traditions, she has dedicated her life to inspiring the next generation of musicians and teachers. Her innovative approach blends Kodály-inspired methods with flexible, creative lesson planning, making music accessible and joyful for all learners.

As a leader in bilingual music education, Chunglien has trained countless teachers and developed a comprehensive curriculum that empowers both students and educators. Her vision is to create a nurturing environment where every child can discover the transformative power of music.

Achievements

  • Founder of Our Music Time, a leading bilingual music education program
  • Decades of teaching experience in both Eastern and Western contexts
  • Developer of innovative, Kodály-inspired curriculum and materials
  • Mentor and trainer for music educators worldwide

Philosophy

"Music is a universal language that bridges cultures and generations. My mission is to make music education joyful, inclusive, and transformative for every child and teacher."

— Chunglien Liu
